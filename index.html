About Me

Welcome to my portfolio page! My name is Roy Reyna and I currently reside in San Antonio, TX. I'm a military kid so I've been all over the country. I moved to Texas after my dad retired in 2005 and I have moved out since. I started off in the Rio Grande Valley, home to some of the best citrus out there, and now I'm enjoying it here in San Antonio!

Some of my hobbies include watching football (#TitanUp!), listening to metalcore and EDM music, and traveling. I am busy body and find it really hard to stay put. I have a beautiful wife of 7 years, no kids, no pets and we're loving our time together. We just purchased a new home and now I'm excited to begin learning Full Stack Web Development in hopes of a career change.

I've been a high school math teacher for the past 8 years teaching subjects like Algebra 2 and AP Calculus. While I do love the math content and the eagerness some of my students exhibit on a daily basis, I feel like it's time to leave the education field. My degree is in Applied Mathematics and I took several coding classes in college because I was on the fence about obtaining a CS degree. As time as gone on, I've wondered more and more about what it would've been like if I continued to code. So now I'm here to find out! I'm excited about what's in store for my future.
